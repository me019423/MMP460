 Less Waste for a Cleaner Planet





Welcome to our initiative to reduce plastic waste in cosmetics and help minimize our environmental impact. Plastic has become a big part of our daily lives and it is hard to resist, it’s everywhere! But plastic pollution has become a global concern and we simply must help reduce it. Only about 9% of plastic has been recycled, 12% has been burned and the remaining 79% has ended up in landfills or the environment. Plastic that is buried in landfills can leach harmful chemicals that spread into groundwater. Plastic pollution has also been linked to global warming due to the emission of greenhouse gasses when plastic is produced or burned. This data is concerning and we consumers must take action, so our planet has a chance to stay healthy and flourish.
